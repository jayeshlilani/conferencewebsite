Paper Submission Guidelines

  • The paper submitted to this conference must be original and must not be under consideration for publication elsewhere
  • The paper should be written in clear, concise and grammatically correct English
  • The paper length should be between 8 to 10 pages and formatted in Microsoft Word (.docx/.doc).
  • Unformatted papers and papers beyond the page limit may not be sent for review process
  • Author name(s), affiliation(s), acknowledgements should be clearly mentioned in the manuscript.
  • It should begin with a title, a short abstract, and a list of key words and its introduction should summarize the contributions of the paper.
  • Electronic submission of paper (in MS-Word.doc, .docx) must be mailed to icestmm19@gmail.com
  • Upon the notification of acceptance, the author will have to submit final copy of the manuscript of the paper, registration fee and registration form on or before the last date.

REVIEW PROCESS

  • Papers will be checked for plagiarism subjected to Double-blind review.
  • Selection criteria include originality of ideas, novelty, correctness, clarity and significance of results and quality of presentation.

Publication

  • Authors are invited to submit the Full Papers, not exceeding 10-pages in MS word format (soft copy) and neatly typed on A-4 size paper.
  • Each manuscript will undergo a peer review process. The contributed papers must be original and should not have been published/accepted in journals/proceedings.
  • Plagiarism in any form is unethical and unacceptable. In order to ensure that the content submitted is original and trustworthy, a similarity check will be done.Upon the notification of acceptance, the author will have to submit final copy of the manuscript of the paper, copyright transfer agreement and registration fee and registration form on or before the last date.
